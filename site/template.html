You can now leverage the power of MJML directly from its first desktop app. Import and manage templates, edit your email with live rendering, customize your environment and send tests straight from the app!

Use the power of MJML, easily.

MJML App support out of the box all MJML features. You can instantly see the result in the preview, and resize it to live see the responsive.

Features

Send your tests to your inbox

The live preview is pretty cool, but we also provided a way to directly send tests to your inbox to visualize the final email on your phone, desktop client or browser client. You will need a Mailjet account, with your API Keys, and an valid email address to be used as a sender, and recipient.
